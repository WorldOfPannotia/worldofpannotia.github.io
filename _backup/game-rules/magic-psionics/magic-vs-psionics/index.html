--- title: Magic vs. Psionics crumbs: [game-rules, magic-psionics] crumbTitles: [Game Rules, Magic & Psionics] ---

For the purpose of mechanics, magic and psionics in Pannotia are considered different. Spell Resistance is ineffective against psionics, and Power Resistance is ineffective against spells. However, many creatures with resistance to one have resistance to the other. See Monsters & Power Resistance for more details.
