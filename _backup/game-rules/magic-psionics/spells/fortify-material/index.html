Transmutation

Level: Clr 2, Sor/Wiz 2

Components: V, S, M

Casting Time: 1 standard action

Range: Touch

Target: Object touched

Duration: Instantaneous

Saving Throw: Fortitude negates (harmless, object)

Spell Resistance: Yes (object)

You are able to increase the resilience of your equipment. The target of this spell has its hardness increased by 3.

Note: Multiple castings of fortify material do not stack. An object's hardness can only be increased by 3 using this spell.

Material Component: A pouch of powder containing a mixture of steel, iron, and adamantine worth 50gp.
