Racial Traits

    * -4 Strength, +2 Dexterity, -2 Constitution. * Small size: +1 bonus to Armor Class, +1 bonus on attack rolls, +4 bonus on _hide_ checks, -4 penalty on grapple checks, lifting and carrying limits ¾ those of Medium characters. * A kobold's base land speed is 30 feet. * Darkvision out to 60 feet. * Racial Skills: A kobold character has a +2 racial bonus on _craft (trapmaking)_, _profession (miner)_, and _search_ checks. * Racial Feats: A kobold character gains feats according to its character class. * +1 natural armor bonus. * Special Qualities (see above): Light sensitivity. * Automatic Languages: Draconic.
