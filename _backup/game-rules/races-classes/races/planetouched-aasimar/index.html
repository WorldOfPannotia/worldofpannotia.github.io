Racial Traits

    * +2 Wisdom, +2 Charisma. * Medium size. * An aasimar's base land speed is 30 feet. * Darkvision: Aasimars can see in the dark up to 60 feet. * Racial Skills: Aasimars have a +2 racial bonus on _spot_ and _listen_ checks. * Racial Feats: An aasimar gains feats according to its class levels. * Special Attacks (see above): Daylight. * Special Qualities (see above): Resistance to acid 5, cold 5, and electricity 5. * Automatic Languages: Common, Celestial. Bonus Languages: Draconic, Dwarven, Elven, Gnome, Halfling, Sylvan. * Favored Class: Paladin. * Level adjustment +1.

    **Daylight (Sp):** An aasimar can use daylight once per day (caster level equal to class levels).
