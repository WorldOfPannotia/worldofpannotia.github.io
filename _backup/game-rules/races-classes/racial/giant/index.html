Description

A giant is a humanoid-shaped creature of great strength, usually of at least Large size.

Racial Features

A(n) Giant possesses the following traits (unless otherwise noted in a creature's entry).
    * Low-light vision. * Proficient with all simple and martial weapons, as well as any natural weapons. * Proficient with whatever type of armor (light, medium or heavy) it is described as wearing, as well as all lighter types. Giants not described as wearing armor are not proficient with armor. Giants are proficient with shields if they are proficient with any form of armor. * Giants eat, sleep, and breathe.
