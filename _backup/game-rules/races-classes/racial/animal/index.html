Description

An animal is a living, nonhuman creature, usually a vertebrate with no magical abilities and no innate capacity for language or culture.

Racial Features

A(n) Animal possesses the following traits (unless otherwise noted in a creature's entry).
    * Intelligence score of 1 or 2 (no creature with an Intelligence score of 3 or higher can be an animal). * Low-light vision. * Alignment: Always neutral. * Treasure: None. * Proficient with its natural weapons only. A noncombative herbivore uses its natural weapons as a secondary attack. Such attacks are made with a -5 penalty on the creature's attack rolls, and the animal receives only ½ its Strength modifier as a damage adjustment. * Proficient with no armor unless trained for war. * Animals eat, sleep, and breathe.
