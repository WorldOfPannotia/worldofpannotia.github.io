Necromancy

Level: Clr 9

Cleric Domains: Ravage 9

Range: Close (25 ft. + 5 ft./2 levels)

Targets: One or more creatures, no two of which can be more than 30 ft. apart

This spell functions like harm, except as noted above. The maximum damage to each creature is 250.
