--- title: "Compressed Spell" slug: "compressed-spell" feat: name: "Compressed Spell" types: ["Metamagic"] description: | You can compress the effects of a spell into a smaller area with higher than normal intensity. benefit: | You can alter a burst, emanation, line, or spread shaped spell to compress its area. Any numeric measurements of the spell's area are reduced by 50%. All variable, numeric effects of an empowered spell are doubled. A compressed spell uses up a spell slot three levels higher than the spell's actual level. Spells that do not have an area of one of these four sorts are not affected by this feat. ---
