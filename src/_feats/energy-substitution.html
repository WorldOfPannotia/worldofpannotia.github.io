When employing a spell with the acid, cold, electricity, fire, or sonic designator, you can modify the spell to use your chosen type of energy instead. The altered spell uses a spell slot of the spell's normal level. The altered spell works normally in all respects except the type of damage dealt. special: | You can gain this feat multiple times.
