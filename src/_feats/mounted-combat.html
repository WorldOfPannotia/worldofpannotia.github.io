--- title: "Mounted Combat" slug: "mounted-combat" feat: name: "Mounted Combat" types: ["General", "Fighter"] prerequisite: | _ride_ 1 rank. benefit: | Once per round when your mount is hit in combat, you may attempt a _ride_ check (as a reaction) to negate the hit. The hit is negated if your _ride_ check result is greater than the opponent's attack roll. (Essentially, the _ride_ check result becomes the mount's Armor Class if it's higher than the mount's regular AC.) special: | A fighter may select Mounted Combat as one of his fighter bonus feats. ---
