--- title: "Glossolalia" spell: schools: - name: "Evocation" subschools: [] descriptors: ["Sonic, Mind-Affecting"] classes: - name: "Bard" abbr: "Brd" level: 2 components: [V] castingTime: "1 standard action" range: "60 ft." area: "Cone-shaped burst" duration: "Instantaneous" savingThrow: "Fortitude partial; see text" spellResistance: "Yes" description: | You utter shrieks of nonsense and gibberish that thinking creatures find distracting and confusing. The spell affects only creatures with Intelligence scores of 3 or higher. Any such creatures within the spell's area must succeed on Fortitude saves or be stunned for 1 round (if they have Intelligence scores of 20 or higher), dazed for 1 round (if they have Intelligence scores between 10 and 19), or shaken for 1 round (if they have Intelligence scores between 3 and 9). In addition, a creature within the spell's area loses any psionic focus it might have had and takes a -4 penalty on Concentration checks for 1 round. These effects happen whether or not the creature succeeded on its save. ---
