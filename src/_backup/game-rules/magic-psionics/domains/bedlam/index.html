Granted Power: Turn or destroy lawful creatures as a good cleric turns undead. Rebuke, command, or bolster chaotic creatures as an evil cleric rebukes undead. Use these abilities a total number of times per day equal to 3 + your Charisma modifier. This granted power is a supernatural ability.

  1. Stop Hitting Yourself
  2. Glitterdust
  3. Stop Hitting Yourself, Improved
  4. Caos Hammer
  5. Stop Hitting Yourself, Mass
  6. Suggestion, Mass
  7. Stop Hitting Yourself, Mass Improved
  8. Symbol of Insanity
  9. Storm of Vengeance
