Psychometabolism

Level: Psychic Warrior 1

Display: Olfactory

Manifesting Time: 1 standard action

Range: Personal

Target: You

Duration: 1 round/level (D)

Power Points: 1

This power causes instant diminution, halving your height, length, and width and dividing your weight by 8. This decrease changes your size category to the next smaller one. You gain a +2 size bonus to Dexterity, a -2 size penalty to Strength (to a minimum effective Strength score of 1), a +1 size bonus on attack rolls, and a +1 size bonus to Armor Class due to your reduced size.
