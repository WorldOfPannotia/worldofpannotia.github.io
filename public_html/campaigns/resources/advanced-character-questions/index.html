Advanced Character Questionnaire

I am rewording many of the qestions to use "your character" and related phrases instead of "you". Please excuse the strange grammar in places where I have not yet fixed a question.

Jump to:

  • Personality/Beliefs

Introduction Questions

  1. Give a two or three word description of your character.
  2. Does your character have any nicknames, street names, titles, nom de plume?
  3. What is your character's full birth name?
  4. Where does your character live?
  5. Why does your character live there?
  6. What is your character's citizenship status?
  7. What is your character's most obvious blessing or strength?
  8. What does your character perceive as his or her greatest strength?
  9. What is your character's most obvious flaw or weakness?
  10. What does your character perceive as his or her greatest weakness?
  11. Was there any event or cause of these weaknesses?

Physical Traits

  1. How old is your character?
  2. What is your character's gender?
  3. What is your character's species/race?
  4. How tall is your character?
  5. How much does your character weigh?
  6. What is your character's general body type, frame, bone structure, and poise?
  7. What is your character's skin colour?
  8. What is your character's hair colour?
  9. What is your character's hair style?
  10. Does your character have any facial hair?
  11. What is your character's eye colour?
  12. Does it change?
  13. How attractive is your character?
  14. What is character's your most distinguishing feature?
  15. Does your character have any scars, tattoos, or birthmarks?
  16. If so, how did your character acquire them?
  17. What do these distinguishing marks look like?
  18. Do they have any special significance?
  19. Where are they located?
  20. What is your character's handedness (left/right)?
  21. Does your character resemble some currently known person?
  22. What is your character's hand size/description?
  23. Does your character wear a uniform?
  24. What kind of clothing does your character wear?
  25. What is your character's clothing style or level of sophistication?
  26. What size is your character for various pieces of clothing?
  27. Does your character wear makeup?
  28. Does your character wear glasses/contacts?
  29. What sort of vocal tone does your character have?
  30. Does your character get sick often? (e.g. Maybe he or she catches the flu twice a year and that's it, or maybe he or she has a weak immune system and catches anything that goes around)

History

  1. Where is your character's homeland?
  2. What are its people like?
  3. Is your character aware of its history?
  4. Is your character patriotic or a social outcast?
  5. What are your character's opinions of home?
  6. Where is your character's home town?
  7. What is your character's home town's name?
  8. What was the area like and how did it affect your character?
  9. Did your character witness any historical events?
  10. If so, how did that event impact him or her?
  11. Are your character's real reasons for becoming an adventurer different from what he or she tells others?
  12. If so, how might such secrets be revealed?
  13. How far would you go to keep such secrets from being revealed? What would you do if the truth became known?
  14. What does your character fear would occur if the truth became known?
  15. Does your character have any particularly high or low ability scores?
  16. If so, were these scores a result of you past?
  17. How have these scores affected your life so far?
  18. Did you affect you in your chosen profession?
  19. What about your race, growing up were you in the majority or a minority?
  20. How were you treated by other nearby races?
  21. Were you persecuted for your race?
  22. Did this impact your outlook in any way?
  23. Did it affect your personality?
  24. How does your character feel about other races?
  25. Does your character have any justification from your past experience for holding such views?
  26. Were there any traumatic experiences in your early years (death of a family member, abandonment, orphaned at an early age, etc.)?
  27. Briefly describe a defining moment in your childhood and how it influenced your life.
  28. What was childhood like for you?
  29. Was it calm and peaceful or turbulent and traumatic?
  30. Did you have any childhood friends?
  31. If so, who and where are they now?
  32. Is your character still close to them or has your character grown apart?
  33. What stupid things did your character do when you were younger?
  34. Which toys from your childhood has your character kept?
  35. Why? What do they mean to you?
  36. If you didn't keep any, why not?
  37. What did your character do to them all?
  38. Does your character have any deep, dark secrets in the past that may come back to haunt you?
  39. What conflicts might arise from your past?
  40. Is your character who you claim to be?
  41. Does your character have any sort of criminal record?
  42. How does your character view the heroes/legends of your country?

Family

  1. Who were your parents?
  2. Were you raised by them?
  3. If not, then why didn't they and who did raise you?
  4. What is your father's full name?
  5. What is your mother's full name?
  6. What did your parents and/or foster parents do for a living?
  7. What was their standing in the community?
  8. Did your family stay in one area or move around a lot?
  9. How did your character get along with their parents?
  10. How would your parents describe you? Answer this in the voice of your mother, then in your father's.
  11. Does your character have any siblings?
  12. If so how many and what were their names?
  13. What was your birth position in the family (i.e.-ast born, and child, rd, etc.)?
  14. How did your character get along with each of their siblings?
  15. What was your family life like?
  16. List all current knowledge of family locations, spouses, children, birth dates, schooling and any important incidents that only you and they might remember.
  17. Are any or all of your family still alive?
  18. If so, where are they now?
  19. Does your character stay in touch with them or has your character become estranged?
  20. Draw out your family tree, including living and dead relatives.
  21. Does your character love or hate one member of the family in particular?
  22. Is any member of the family special to you in any way (perhaps, as a confidant, mentor, or arch-rival)?
  23. Are there any black (or white) sheep in the family (including you)?
  24. If so, who are they and how did they "gain" the position?
  25. If this person is not you, then how does your character feel about them?
  26. Does your character have a notorious or celebrated ancestor?
  27. If so, what did this person do to become famous or infamous?
  28. What do people assume about you once your ancestry is revealed? Does your character try to live up to the reputation of their ancestor, try to live it down, or ignore it?
  29. Has your character begun your own family?
  30. If not, does your character ever want to have a family of your own someday?
  31. If so, with who or what type of person?
  32. What type of person would be your ideal mate?
  33. What would you be willing to do to protect such a person?
  34. Is there anything you wouldn't do to protect such a person and if so what?
  35. Would anything change your mind on this issue and if so, what?

Relationships

  1. Does your character have any close friends?
  2. If so, who and what are they like?
  3. What is the history of their relationship(s) with you?
  4. Does your character currently have a best friend whom you would protect with your reputation or your life?
  5. If so, who are they and what caused you to feel so close to them? What would have to happen for you to end this relationship?
  6. Does your character have any bitter enemies?
  7. If so, who are they, what are they like, and what is the history of their feud with you?
  8. Has your character defeated them before?
  9. How might these enemies seek to discomfit you in the future?
  10. What valuable or important contacts does your character have?
  11. How did your character come to know them?
  12. Which person(s) or group(s) is your character most loyal to?
  13. List any past serious relationships that you have had, and give a brief overview of the relationship(s).
  14. How does your character think others generally perceive you?
  15. If someone crossed your path, what would you do?
  16. Who is your most trusted ally?
  17. Who does your character trust, in general?
  18. Who does your character despise and why?
  19. Name seven things you hate in others.
  20. Is your image consistent?
  21. Do people see you in similar ways?
  22. Does your character deliberately present yourself differently in different situations, and how?
  23. For what would you die for?
  24. For whom would you go to extremes for?
  25. Who does you turn to when you're in trouble?
  26. What is the worst thing someone has done to you?
  27. What are your general reaction to an attractive member of the opposite sex who lets you know they are available?
  28. How does your character get along with others in the same field and/or work environment?
  29. How can you be blackmailed, beaten, and tricked (by PCs or NPCs)?
  30. Has your character lost any loves?
  31. How did your character handle the situation (short & long term)?
  32. Who would miss you, should you go missing?
  33. Who might protect you?
  34. Who might be convinced to sell you out?
  35. How close is your character to your friends?
  36. What do they know about you?
  37. What do they not know about you?
  38. What does your character know and not know about them?
  39. Does your character live with anyone (housemates, roommates, relatives, friends, near-strangers, family friend, spouse, boyfriend, girlfriend, lover)?
  40. Is your character a member of any special interest groups?
  41. What is your level of involvement?
  42. What is your current status with local law-enforcement?
  43. Does your character have a record of cooperation or non-cooperation with authorities?
  44. Does your character have a file with local, national or internation law enforcement?

Personality/Beliefs

  1. Does your character, or did you, have any role models?
  2. Does your character have any heroes or idols, either contemporary or from legend?
  3. If so, has your character ever met them?
  4. Did you ever become disillusioned with former heroes or idols? If so, why and what were the circumstances?
  5. When did your character decide to become an adventurer?
  6. Why has your character chosen to risk your life as a career?
  7. What does your character expect to get out of being an adventurer (satisfaction, glory, money, redemption, etc.)? What, if anything, would make them stop adventuring (death of a loved one, marriage, mutilation, money, position, old age, etc.)?
  8. Does your character have any dreams or ambitions?
  9. If not, why?
  10. How does adventuring fit into this dream, or does it at all?
  11. What are your short term goals (what would you like to be doing within a year)?
  12. What are your long term goals (what would you like to be doing twenty years from now)?
  13. If these goals seem at odds with each other or with your dreams, how does your character reconcile the differences?
  14. How does your character seek to fulfill these dreams, goals, & ambitions?
  15. Does your character have any great rational or irrational fears or phobias? If so, what are the origins of or reasons behind them?
  16. What, if anything, would it take for you to be able to overcome this?
  17. How does your character react when this fear manifests itself?
  18. Is your character willing to discuss, or even admit to, the situation?
  19. What are your attitudes regarding material wealth?
  20. Is your character miserly with your share of the wealth, or does your character spend it freely?
  21. Is your character greedy or generous?
  22. Does your character see wealth as a mark of success, or just as a means to an end?
  23. How does your character generally treat others?
  24. Does your character trust easily (perhaps too easily) or not?
  25. Is your character introverted (shy and withdrawn) or extroverted (outgoing)?
  26. Is your character a humble soul or blusteringly proud?
  27. Does your character act differently than you feel (concealing your true thoughts)?
  28. What habits would you find most annoying in friends?
  29. Is there any race, creed, alignment, religion, class, profession, political viewpoint, or the like against which you is strongly prejudiced, and why?
  30. How do others typically react to you?
  31. Why, in your opinion, do they act that way?
  32. What are your most annoying habits?
  33. What is your favourite food?
  34. What is your favourite drink?
  35. What is your favourite treat (desert)?
  36. Does your character favour a particular cuisine?
  37. Does your character savor the tastes when eating or "wolf down" your food?
  38. Does your character like food mild or heavily spiced?
  39. Are there any specific foodstuffs that you find disgusting or refuse to eat?
  40. Is your character allergic to any food?
  41. What are your favourite colour(s)?
  42. Is there any colour that you dislike?
  43. Does your character have a favourite (or hated) song, type of music, or instrument?
  44. What is your favourite bard song?
  45. If you have a favourite scent, what is it?
  46. What is your favourite type of animal?
  47. Why does your character category that creature?
  48. Does your character have an animal totem or affinity, and if so for what?
  49. Is there a certain type of animal that you hate or fear?
  50. Is your character allergic to any kinds of animals?
  51. Does your character have any allergies?
  52. Is there anything that enrages you?
  53. Is there anything which embarrasses you?
  54. How does your character react to being teased about it?
  55. Does your character enjoy "roughing it", or does your character prefer your creature comforts?
  56. Does your character believe in the gods or not?
  57. Does your character have a patron Deity?
  58. Is your character devout or impious?
  59. Does your character actively worship and proselytize or does your character simply pay lip service?
  60. What lengths would your character go to defend your faith?
  61. Was your faith influenced or molded by anyone special?
  62. Does your character belong to the orthodox church, or a fringe element thereof (and is the group accepted, frowned upon, or considered heretics)?
  63. How has this impacted your faith and life?
  64. Is your church an accepted religion where your character grew up or did it have to conduct its services in secret?
  65. How did this affect your faith and life?
  66. Has your character ever been persecuted for your faith?
  67. If so, when and how did your character handle it?
  68. Can your character kill?
  69. When did your character decide (or learn) that they could?
  70. What happened and how did your character handle it?
  71. When does your character consider it okay to kill (under what circumstances)?
  72. When does your character consider it wrong to kill (under what circumstances)?
  73. What would your character do if someone else attempted to (or successfully did) kill under their "wrong" circumstances, what would be your reaction?
  74. What if it were your enemy?
  75. What if it were your friend?
  76. What if it were an innocent?
  77. What if the opponent were not in control of their own actions (i.e.-under duress, charmed, dominated, possessed, etc.)?
  78. What would your character do if someone shot at (attacked) you?
  79. What would your character do if something were stolen from you?
  80. What would your character do if you were badly insulted publicly?
  81. What would your character do if a good friend or relative were killed by means other than natural death?
  82. What is the one task your character would absolutely refuse to do?
  83. What does your character consider to be the worst crime someone could commit and why?
  84. How does your character feel about government (rulers) in general?
  85. Why does your character feel that way?
  86. Does your character support the current government of your homeland?
  87. If so, how far is your character willing to go to defend the government? If not, does your character actively oppose it?
  88. Does your character belong to an anti-government organization?
  89. If so, describe the group and it's aims.
  90. What form of government does your character believe is the best (democracy, monarchy, anarchy, aristocratic rule, oligarchy, matriarchy) and why?
  91. Has your character ever been persecuted for their political stance?
  92. If so, describe the occurrence and how it affected you?
  93. Is your character a member of any non-religious group, cause, order, or organization?
  94. If so describe it, its goals, and membership.
  95. How loyal is your character to this group and why?
  96. How did your character become a member?
  97. If you are a former member, did your character leave voluntarily or involuntarily and why?
  98. Was it under good (amicable) conditions or bad?
  99. Is your character being sought or hunted by the organization?
  100. If so, by whom and with what intent (to murder you, to force your return through blackmail or coercion, to spy on your character and make sure they do not reveal any of the groups secrets)?
  101. Does your character have any unusual habits or dominant personality traits that are evident to others?
  102. If so, describe them and how your character acquired them, as well as when they might be more noticeable and what causes them?
  103. How does your character react if made fun of for any of these things?
  104. Does your character have any unusual or nervous mannerisms, such as when talking, thinking, afraid, under stress, or when embarrassed?
  105. If so, are there any reasons behind them from your past?
  106. Does your character have an unusual gait or accent?
  107. If so, where did your character acquire them?
  108. Are there any circumstances where they become more (or less) evident?
  109. How does your character feel and react if made fun of for any of these things?
  110. What is your favourite colour?
  111. What place would your character most like to visit?
  112. What annoys your character the most?
  113. What (if any) are your favourite forms of art?
  114. What is your most treasured possession?
  115. What things could he/ she not live without?
  116. Does your character have a good luck charm?
  117. If your life were to end in 24 hours, what 5 things would your character do in those remaining hours?
  118. Does your character have a "Black-and-White" view of reality or can your character see shades of grey?
  119. Does your character prefer to tear down or build up?
  120. Is your character deliberate or spontaneous?
  121. Does your character prefer the town or the country?
  122. If made to decide, would your character rather be deaf or blind? Why?
  123. How does your character feel about magic, myth, and the supernatural?
  124. Does your character remember your dreams?
  125. Describe a typical dream your character might have.
  126. Describe your worst nightmares.

Career/Training

  1. Where and how were you educated?
  2. Who trained your character in your class or job?
  3. What was your relationship with your teacher(s)/mentor(s)?
  4. How did your character happen across this teacher or mentor?
  5. Was your mentor kind, stern, cruel, indifferent?
  6. Is this person or institution still in existence?
  7. Were you forced into your profession by parents or peers?
  8. Did circumstances dictate your choice of profession?
  9. Were you a prize student or did your character just barely pass?
  10. Look at your skills. How did your character acquire them (especially the unusual ones)?
  11. Has your character ever done anything else for a living?
  12. How does your character function in combat (maneuvers, weaknesses)?
  13. How would your character best be defeated?
  14. Has your character ever received any awards or honours?
  15. What has your character done that was considered "outstanding" in your occupation by others in your field?
  16. What are your long-term goals in work?
  17. Describe any traumatic experiences in your present occupation that has affected your character deeply in some way.
  18. How do your relatives and friends view your present occupation?
  19. Is there anything that you don't currently know how to do that you wish you could?
  20. Is your character envious of others who can do such things in a good-natured way or are they sullen and morose about it?

Lifestyle/Hobbies

  1. What is your normal daily routine?
  2. How does your character feel and react when this routine is interrupted for some reason?
  3. What are your hobbies when they are not adventuring or training?
  4. What would your character do if you had insomnia and had to find something to do to amuse yourself?
  5. What does your character do for relaxation? What things do they do for enjoyment? What interests does your character have?
  6. How does your character normally dress, when not in your adventuring gear?
  7. Does your character like to dress up or down to relax?
  8. Does your character have a look or style? If so, describe it.
  9. What does your character normally wear in bed at home?
  10. What does your character normally wear in bed while adventuring?
  11. Does your character wear any identifiable jewelry?
  12. Where does your character normally put your weapons, magic items, or other valuables when you are sleeping?
  13. What morning or evening routines does your character normally have?
  14. Do these change when you is adventuring?
  15. What pastime (that you participate in regularly) gives you the most enjoyment?
  16. What pastime (that you participate in regularly) gives you the least enjoyment?
  17. Does your character read the newspaper?
  18. If so, which sections and how often?
  19. What is his/ her routine?
  20. Travel: How does your character get around locally?
  21. What is your idea of a good evening's entertainment?
  22. Does your character get seasick, airsick, motion sick (auto or animal)?
  23. Does your character like riding animals?
  24. Any fears in traveling?
  25. What sorts of general belongings or equipment does your character take when traveling?
  26. What are your hangout places?
  27. Does your character go to a bar after work?
  28. Does your character play pool?
  29. Does your character go dancing? Who goes with you?
  30. What does your character read? Scientific textbooks, historical novels, myths and legends, maps, cookbooks, romances, news magazines, science fiction, fantasy, horror, the newspaper, short stories?
  31. What music does your character like?
  32. Does your character have a favourite artist, band or bard?
  33. Will you listen to or sing the same songs over and over, or does it drive you nuts when people do that?
  34. How does your character exercise? Work out at the gym, walks in the morning, run marathons, play sports, couch potato?
  35. Where does your character live?
  36. Does your character rent or own?
  37. Apartment, house or castle?
  38. How close are the neighbors?
  39. Is it a good neighborhood?
  40. What colour is the house?
  41. Which floor is your character on?
  42. Does your character have a lawn?
  43. What about a flower garden?
  44. Does your house have an attic or basement?
  45. What does your furniture look like?
  46. Does your character buy antiques?
  47. What are your walls covered with? Wallpaper, art, photos?
  48. What sorts of curtains does your character have? Frilly lacy ones, Venetian blinds, pull-down shades?
  49. Does your character keep your house clean?
  50. Is it dusty?
  51. Is the bathtub moldy or coated in rust?
  52. Does your character clean it herself?
  53. What do your desk or workspace look like? Small and cramped, huge and expansive, covered in drifts of books and papers?, neatly ordered and clean?
  54. Can you find what you're looking for when you need it?
  55. What colour are your sheets?
  56. Satin or cotton?
  57. Patterned with flowers, or covered with pictures of toy robots?
  58. What sorts of food does your character eat?
  59. Does your character cook your own dinners?
  60. Is your character a good cook, a gourmet, or a terrible cook?
  61. Does your character eat out?
  62. Is your character on a diet?
  63. Where does your character vacation, and how often?
  64. Does your character have any pets?
  65. Does your character keep a calendar or address book?
  66. Where does your character keep it?
  67. Did you leave a Will?
  68. What does it say?

Miscellaneous

  1. What would you like to be remembered for after your death?
  2. What kind of threat does your character present to public?
  3. If your features were to be destroyed beyond recognition, is there any other way of identifying your body?
  4. How much is a pint of mead?
  5. As a player, if you could, what advice would you give you? Speak as if he/she were sitting right here in front of you. Use proper tone so they might heed your advice...
