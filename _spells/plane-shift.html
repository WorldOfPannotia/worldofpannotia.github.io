The size and metal type dictates to which plane of existence or alternate dimension the spell sends the affected creatures." description: | You move yourself or some other creature to another plane of existence or alternate dimension. If several willing persons link hands in a circle, as many as eight can be affected by the plane shift at the same time. From the Material Plane, you can reach any other plane, though you appear 5 to 500 miles (5d%) from your intended destination. **Note:** Plane shift transports creatures instantaneously and then ends.
