--- title: "Craft Cognizance Crystal" slug: "craft-cognizance-crystal" feat: name: "Craft Cognizance Crystal" types: ["Item Creation"] description: | You can create psionic _cognizance crystals_ that store power points. prerequisite: | Manifester level 3rd. benefit: | You can create a _cognizance crystal_. Doing so takes one day for each 1,000 gp in its base price. The base price of a _cognizance crystal_ is equal to the highest-level power it could manifest using all its stored power points, squared, multiplied by 1,000 gp. To create a _cognizance crystal_, you must spend 1/25 of its base price in XP and use up raw materials costing one-half its base price. ---
