--- title: "Power Critical" slug: "power-critical" feat: name: "Power Critical" types: ["General", "Fighter"] prerequisite: | Weapon focus (chosen weapon), base attack bonus +4 benefit: | When using the weapon you selected, you gain a +4 bonus on the roll to confirm a threat. special: | A fighter may select Power Critical as one of his fighter bonus feats. You can gain Power Critical multiple times. Each time you take the feat, it may be with a different weapon or the same weapon. If you take it with the same weapon, the effects of the feats stack. ---
